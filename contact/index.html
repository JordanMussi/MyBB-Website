MyBB Support

Having trouble with your MyBB forum? Please check the MyBB Documentation for an answer before asking your question in the support forums.

MyBB Suggestions and Feedback

Provide feedback and suggest improvements for the MyBB forum software through the appropriate Suggestions and Feedback forum within the Development discussion forums.

MyBB Security Issues

If you've found a potential security vulnerability in MyBB, please report the issue within the Private Inquiries forum.

Get in touch regarding MyBB.com

All issues and feedback relating to the MyBB website and the community forums can be posted in the MyBB.com Community and Site Issues forum.

If you have an issue relating to your account on the Community Forums or other personal matters, please post in the Private Inquiries forum.
